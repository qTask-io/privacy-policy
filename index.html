Privacy Policy for the qTask App

Data Collection

The qTask app collects the following types of data:

  • Email Addresses: The emails used by users to log into the App.
  • Text Data: Task titles, descriptions, and dates converted from voice recordings.

  • Service Improvement: Analyzing data to enhance functionality and user experience.
  • User Support: Resolving technical issues and responding to user inquiries.
